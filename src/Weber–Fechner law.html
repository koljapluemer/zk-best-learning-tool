Weber–Fechner law


- perceived intensity/quantity grows with (more or less) the logarithm of actual quantity/intensity
- as in:
- a point cloud of 20 points looks a bit bigger than one with 10 points.
- you can't see the difference between 120 and 110 points at all
- is this too low level or does this have actual implications for, e.g. interface design?
